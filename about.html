The Story

Kirikou Akili

A Journey of Resilience

Kirikou Akili, whose real name is Youssufu Akbar Niyonkuru, is a Burundian rapper and singer from Ngozi known for blending Afrobeats, Bongo Flava, and hip-hop with a fast rap style.

His story is a powerful journey from difficult childhood conditions and street life to becoming one of Burundi’s most recognized contemporary artists.
